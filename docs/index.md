# Catford Green

Catford Green is a modern, up-market residential community in the heart of Catford Lewisham, set beside Ladywell Fields and moments from Catford and Catford Bridge stations. This site is a resident-led hub for sharing local knowledge, building know-how, and ways to make the most of living here.

## For residents
- Guides on building essentials: filters, bulbs, radiators, ventilation, parcel storage, and waste areas.
- Local recommendations: reliable plumbers, electricians, handypeople, cleaners, and appliance repair.
- Family-friendly picks: after-school clubs, holiday camps, playgrounds, and green spaces.
- Moving in or letting out? Checklists, FAQs, and contacts to help you settle quickly.

## Residents associations
- Estate management: Firstport manages all Catford Green buildings and the wider estate.
- Residents associations: Dixie Court is active; other courts are encouraged to form one. See the guide: [residents-association.md](residents-association.md)
